Nina Yuchi is a Chinese-American graphic design student with not-so-subtle obsessions with pranking her roommates and pineapple on pizza.

Originally from Maryland (“right in between Baltimore and DC!”), she is currently at the Rhode Island School of Design. Nina designs for RISD’s Art History department, RISD Continuing Education, and Brown University’s post- magazine. She is particularly interested in experimental web design and the digital interface.

This website was designed and coded by her. Fonts in use are Termina and Interstate Mono.
