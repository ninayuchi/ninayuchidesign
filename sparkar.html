Intro to SparkAR

Digital design, Augmented Reality



Part of my journey in understanding of augmented reality and AR software was understanding how to relay what I learned. I focused on SparkAR, software used by Facebook and Instagram, to better understand how AR “filters” are implemented and used to relay information, bridge connections, and develop entertainment. I created this Instagram-friendly design to inform viewers about the history of AR and how to navigate SparkAR’s interface. I also wrote more detailed instructions on how to make an AR filter on Instructables, found here.
