post- Magazine 2019

Publication design, branding



post- magazine is a weekly publication in the Brown Daily Herald newspaper, centering student narratives and arts & culture. Every year, we release a full-spread magazine to highlight Brown University’s music event Spring Weekend. In 2019, the University invited artists including Aminé, Daniel Caesar, Yaeji, and Mitski. My co-chief of layout Jacob Lee and I branded and designed the magazine, creating a unique identity that represents both the magazine’s dynamic personality and the Spring Weekend artists’ unique energies.
