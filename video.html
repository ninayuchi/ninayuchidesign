Various videos as studio assignments and personal projects.

In this video, I decided to inject as much connotation and context as I could into a process that is used universally. After tying several of my own knots, I realized the number of times you loop the string/rope it is arbitrary. I decided to explore this idea of intense arbitrariness by showing the steps of tying a slip knot with several loops until the end shows the knot being undone with a simple tug of one end. Made with Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro.

A video of a keyboard prototype exploring the defamiliarization of typing out a text message then deleting it. The keyboard disables the delete button and automatically sends out messages after ten seconds, forcing the user to send whatever they initially typed out regardless of the consequences. Made with Premiere Pro.

A personal project documenting my weekend trip to New York City with friends. Made with Premiere Pro.

“Passerby” revolves around the story of two students who always hear descriptions and rumors of each other but have always passed each other by. This film was made with Basia Bieniek, Elisha Gillette, and Natalie Paik. Made with Photoshop and Premiere Pro.
