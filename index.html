About

Nina is a Chinese-American graphic designer who can't stop thinking about her next hair color. She is interested in experimental web design and its intersection with social change & identity.

Originally from Maryland (“right in between Baltimore and DC!”), she is currently at the Rhode Island School of Design. Nina designs for RISD’s Art History department, RISD Continuing Education, and Brown University’s post- magazine.

This website was designed and coded by her with HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery. Fonts in use are Nimbus Sans Extended and Simplon Mono.
