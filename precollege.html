I have the privilege of working as a graphic designer for RISD’s Continuing Education office. One of my recent projects consisted of designing the banners for RISD’s Pre-College info day. The banners were printed and displayed around RISD’s campus.

The most challenging aspects of this series of banners were understanding how it fits into the newly developed Pre-College identity and how it will affect the overall RISD identity which is fluid and always changing. Since the program is for high schoolers, the designs and colors reflect a playful and more whimsical approach to an art college experience.
