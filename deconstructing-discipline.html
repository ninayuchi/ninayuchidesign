Deconstructing Discipline

Web design, web development, research



Deconstructing Discipline is an ongoing project inspired by my interest in speculative, critical, and inclusive design. The website takes from Dunne and Raby’s mapping for speculative design: in between the present and future, not too weird and not too safe. Using these labels as a starting point, users are invited to map out their own definitions of speculative design in relation to the creator’s initial map. Users are also able to upload their own images and change the axis labels. Thus they begin to deconstruct their own design discipline through navigating a plethora of existing works.
