About The Project

Deconstructing Discipline is an ongoing project by Nina Yuchi. The website draws inspiration from Dunne and Raby’s mapping for speculative design: in between the present and future, not too weird and not too safe. Using these labels as a starting point, users are invited to map out their own definitions of speculative design in relation to the creator’s initial map. Users are also able to upload their own images and change the axis labels. Thus they begin to deconstruct their own design discipline through navigating a plethora of existing works.

This website was coded by Nina Yuchi using HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Note: works best on desktop. Not optimized for responsive web.
