Vote, or Die
Digital design, social media campaign, research


To create a social media design encouraging young Asian-Americans to vote in the 2018 elections, I decided to focus on consolidating young Asian-Americans’ sense of identity within the United States. Vote, or Die appropriates Benjamin Franklin’s political cartoon "Join, or Die" with an Asian dragon instead of the snake, bringing in familiar imagery of both historical American illustrations and traditional Asian symbols.

I also created a second piece that appropriates the historically negative phrase “yellow peril” that white Americans used to demonize Asian immigrants and introduced the expression “Power over Peril”.

I later posted this on my personal Instagram account.
