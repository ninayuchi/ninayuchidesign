Nina Yuchi




Typography in Oaxaca

Handwritten typography, publication, bookbinding



In January 2020, I travelled to Oaxaca, Mexico as a part of the RISD Global Wintersession program for the course Mexico: Color, Mestizaje, and Design Futures. During my time there, I was enamored by the city's hand-painted posters, ornamental shop signs, and sprawling graffiti. Throughout the course, I collected images of interesting typography and replicated them in this book. I presented these spreads to my classmates as well as local visiting artists.
